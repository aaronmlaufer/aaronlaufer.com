Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This policy describes how the applications hosted on this domain and its subdomains handle your information. These are personal projects, built and operated by one person.

Information We Collect

When you sign in through OAuth (e.g. Google, GitHub, or a similar provider), the application receives the basic profile information the provider shares with us — typically your name, email address, profile image, and a stable user identifier.

If the application requests additional scopes (such as read access to specific resources in your account), the consent screen shown by the provider will list them explicitly. We only request the scopes the application needs to function.

How We Use It

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • To personalize the application (e.g. show your name or avatar).
  • To provide the features the application advertises at sign-in.

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third parties for marketing. We do not use it to train machine-learning models.

Storage and Security

Tokens issued by OAuth providers are stored encrypted and used only to make API calls on your behalf. We take reasonable measures to protect this data, but no system is perfectly secure.

Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you revoke access from your OAuth provider's settings page, or email us to request deletion, we will remove your account data within 30 days.

Cookies

The applications use cookies strictly necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Children

These applications are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you in the application.

Contact

For questions or deletion requests, please use the contact method provided within the application you signed in to.
